Hotels Are Nice. Or You Could Go Camping.

Los Gatos

We've reserved a block of rooms at the Toll House hotel. There are a limited number of rooms, so it pays to book sooner rather than later.

When booking online, use GSWD69 in the group code. If it shows there are no rooms available, please call (408.395.7070) to confirm this. The online system sometimes misses rooms (sorry!)

If the rooms from the block are all booked up, use your AAA discount! There are also a couple other hotels in Los Gatos that are pretty close to the Toll House

Santa Cruz

We're not setting up any room blocks here, but Santa Cruz is still pretty close to Radonich Ranch. We'd rather you stay closeby so that you can easily be a part of the festivities on Friday and Sunday, but if that's what you want to do...

San Francisco

We really don't suggest staying in San Francisco. You'll be far from the festivities and we wouldn't want you to miss anything!

If you're planning on arriving in the bay area early or sticking around after the wedding, we'd be happy to suggest places to stay.
